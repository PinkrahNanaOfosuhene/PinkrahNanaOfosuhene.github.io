Applied Climatology

Tourism Climate Comfort Index in Ghana

A station-based applied climatology project assessing climate suitability for tourism planning across Ghana.

Objective

Evaluate seasonal and regional tourism climate suitability across Ghana.

Datasets

Long-term Ghana Meteorological Agency station records from 22 synoptic stations.

Methods

Tourism Climate Comfort Index computation, data imputation, and spatial interpolation.

Outputs

Monthly comfort index plots, station heatmaps, and Ghana-wide spatial comfort maps.

Research Relevance

This work is retained as applied climatology because it uses meteorological station data to support climate-sensitive planning. It is secondary to the severe-weather, hydroclimatology, and climate-extremes portfolio.
