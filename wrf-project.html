Numerical Weather Prediction

WRF Simulation of the July 2022 Central Appalachian Flood

A WRF/WPS event simulation using ERA5 boundary conditions, a nested model setup, and NOAA Stage IV precipitation verification.

Objective

Simulate the July 26-27, 2022 Central Appalachian flood event and evaluate modeled precipitation.

Datasets

ERA5 initial and boundary conditions; NOAA Stage IV precipitation for verification.

Methods

WRF/WPS preprocessing, nested domains, physics configuration, HPC execution, WRF-Python verification.

Outputs

Accumulated precipitation maps, model-observation comparison, and event interpretation.

Research Relevance

The project demonstrates numerical modeling workflow competency for high-impact hydroclimate events and provides process-level context for extreme rainfall over complex Appalachian terrain.
