Hydroclimatology

Kentucky Mesonet vs NOAA Atlas 14

Precipitation-frequency statistics from Kentucky Mesonet observations compared with NOAA Atlas 14 design-rainfall guidance.

Objective

Evaluate how Kentucky Mesonet precipitation-frequency estimates compare with NOAA Atlas 14.

Datasets

Kentucky Mesonet observations and NOAA Atlas 14 reference precipitation-frequency estimates.

Methods

Annual maxima, return-period estimation, IDF curves, and spatial comparison of precipitation intensity.

Outputs

IDF curves, spatial rainfall diagnostics, and hydroclimate design interpretation.

Research Relevance

This project supports regional hydroclimate risk assessment by testing whether modern Mesonet observations reveal short-duration precipitation behavior that differs from established Atlas 14 design guidance.
